Status: Draft content for integration, staging, and product review.

These terms are published so Proximity clients can load realistic legal content during development and pre-release testing. They describe the intended structure of the service agreement, but they are still subject to legal review and may be replaced before any production launch.

By using a build that references this repository, you acknowledge that the service may still be experimental, that individual features may change quickly, and that this draft is not yet the final public agreement for the service.

You may use the service only if you are legally permitted to do so under the laws that apply to you. If the product asks you to confirm age or eligibility requirements, you must provide accurate information.

You are responsible for keeping access to your device and any linked sign-in method reasonably secure. You must not share account access in a way that would misrepresent identity, bypass safety controls, or allow another person to act as you without permission.

## 2. Acceptable use

You may use the service only for lawful and legitimate social discovery purposes supported by the product.

You must not use the service to stalk, harass, threaten, extort, deceive, impersonate, scrape, or profile other users in a way that would violate their rights or reasonable expectations. You must not attempt to infer another user's precise location beyond what the product intentionally reveals.

You must not interfere with the operation of the service, attempt to access non-public systems, or use automation to abuse matching, messaging, moderation, or account creation flows.

## 3. User content and profile information

You remain responsible for profile text, optional photos, status information, and other content you provide through the service. You should upload only content that you have the right to use and that is consistent with community and safety rules.

You grant Proximity a limited right to host, process, adapt, and display submitted content only as needed to operate, secure, review, and improve the service. This draft license is intended to support product functionality, moderation, and abuse prevention rather than any broader commercial reuse.

## 4. Service availability

Because the product may still be in active development, features may be introduced, modified, rate-limited, interrupted, or removed without advance notice. We may temporarily suspend access for maintenance, operational risk, safety investigation, or legal compliance.

We may also change geographic availability, supported devices, or authentication methods while the service is being prepared for wider release.

## 5. Safety and enforcement

We may investigate suspected misuse, restrict feature access, remove content, or suspend accounts where necessary to protect users, respond to complaints, enforce platform rules, or comply with legal obligations.

Where practical, enforcement decisions may consider the severity of the conduct, repeated violations, attempts to evade prior restrictions, and the risk posed to other users or to the service itself.

## 6. Feedback and early product participation

If you provide product feedback, bug reports, translation suggestions, or usability notes, Proximity may use that feedback to improve the product without any obligation to compensate you. This is included because the repository is intended to support pre-release product work rather than a fully finalized public launch.
