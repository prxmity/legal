# Proximity Community Guidelines

Status: Draft content for integration, staging, and product review.

These guidelines exist to give the product a realistic policy document for onboarding, in-app review screens, and moderation-related UI while the final wording is still being refined.

## 1. Respect other people

Treat other users with respect. Do not harass, intimidate, threaten, shame, or repeatedly contact someone after they have indicated they do not want to continue interacting.

## 2. No deceptive behavior

Do not impersonate another person, misrepresent material facts about yourself, or use the product to trick users into unsafe, manipulative, or exploitative interactions.

## 3. No abusive or illegal use

Do not use the service for hate, exploitation, extortion, stalking, trafficking, non-consensual sexual content, or any activity that would violate applicable law or create a serious safety risk.

## 4. Location sensitivity

Because the product may present nearby people or related distance signals, use extra care with anything that could expose, infer, or pressure someone about where they are or where they may go next.

## 5. Reporting and enforcement

We may review reports, investigate misuse, limit product access, remove content, or suspend accounts to protect users and the service.

## 6. Draft status

This is a draft guideline set intended for product testing and review. Final production rules may expand on safety categories, reporting mechanics, and enforcement detail.
