# Prxmity Community Guidelines

Effective date: 2026-06-19

Prxmity is a location-based, adult-only social-discovery service. These Community Guidelines explain the behavior standards expected from everyone who uses the service.

These Guidelines supplement the Terms of Service. If you break them, we may warn you, restrict features, suspend your account, or remove you from the service.

## 1. Treat people with respect

Do not harass, shame, threaten, intimidate, pressure, or repeatedly pursue another person after they show they do not want contact or interaction.

## 2. No stalking or unsafe pursuit

Do not use Prxmity to follow, monitor, corner, pressure, or repeatedly approach another person in a way that feels invasive, unsafe, or coercive.

Do not use nearby or radar-style features to track another person beyond the intended social-discovery use of the service.

## 3. No deception or impersonation

Do not impersonate another person, misrepresent material facts about yourself, or use the service to manipulate people into unsafe or exploitative situations.

## 4. No abuse of location features

Because Prxmity uses location-sensitive features, you must not:

- try to infer another user's exact routine, home, workplace, or repeated whereabouts
- archive, export, scrape, or build your own tracking history about other users
- use the service for surveillance, coercion, or control

## 5. No exploitation, violence, or illegal use

Do not use the service for hate, exploitation, extortion, trafficking, non-consensual sexual conduct, threats, violence, fraud, or any other unlawful or seriously unsafe activity.

## 6. Respect boundaries and consent

If another user does not want to engage, back off.

Do not pressure people for in-person meetings, personal details, sexual conduct, or continued interaction. Do not use location knowledge or proximity as leverage.

## 7. Respect the service

Do not abuse the service technically or operationally. This includes scraping, automation, bypassing controls, probing non-public systems, or interfering with normal operation.

## 8. Reporting and enforcement

We may investigate reports, review misuse, restrict features, remove content, suspend accounts, or permanently ban users where reasonably necessary to protect people or the service.

We may also act without waiting for a formal report if we detect serious safety, abuse, or integrity risks.

To report harassment, stalking, unsafe conduct, impersonation, or other misuse, contact: `support@prxmity.com`

## 9. Adults only

Prxmity is only for users aged 18 and older. Do not use the service if you are under 18.

## 10. Changes to these Guidelines

We may update these Guidelines from time to time. Material changes may also be reflected in related legal or product documents.
